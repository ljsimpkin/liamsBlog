Neuroplasticity

What is it? Neuro = brain plasticity = the quality of being easily molded. Neuroplasticity is the idea that our brains are easily molded, capable of continuously making new structures. In the past, it was believed that our brains remain fixed for most of our adult life.

This is a hugely important concept because it means you have the power to change your brain today. Our lives are constantly changing, they are not fixed. You can shape them in positive and negative ways.

To some extent I engage with this already. I am very careful about what content I consume, I like to continuously learn things. From reading this, I will engage in more reflective writing, thinking about how I did and how I learnt something, what can improve.

Growth Mindset

A growth mindset is a mindset that everything is possible, where you thrive on challenges, and failure doesn’t define your ability but instead creates an opportunity to grow and learn.
