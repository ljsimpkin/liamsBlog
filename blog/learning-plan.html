My Learning Plan

20 Apr 2022

My goal is to work in the product development space, building technology, and to help manage the business and people’s interests.

My goal at Dev Academy is to develop software skills for building technology.

My future goal is to invest in startups.

I am quite inspired by the technology space, building tools, that sort of thing. I would love to help with the creation of technology companies and their products. Software engineering is a fun and common element of most new technologies. It teaches you how to problem solve and has unlimited potential for scale. On more of an intrinsic level, I enjoy the ability to be able to build things. It’s awesome getting excited by an idea and knowing you have the capability to bring it into the world. When I was younger, I loved playing with lego and it’s a similar feeling I get when I build something with software.

I believe I have what it takes. I am great at learning new things and motivated by the topic. My weakness is self doubt. I would like to develop more mindful skills. To be calm, present, and prevent my mind from wandering off track.

My commitment is this. I stand for many of the core values Dev Academy has; I strive to create a positive environment and be kind and patient with others. I will ask for help when I feel stuck/ or when I go over the time I allocate for myself, and take a break to rest when I'm feeling burnt out.

From the facilitators, I also expect them to strive to create a positive environment and be kind and patient. To be receptive to questions and communicate with ease.

Thank you!
